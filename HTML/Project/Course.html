Courses

Marwadi University offers 46+ programs leading to undergraduate and postgraduate degrees, diplomas as well as doctoral degrees in the fields of Engineering and Technology, Science, Commerce, Management, Law, etc., to name just a few.

At the undergraduate level, the programs that are available at Marwadi University are B.E., B. Arch., BBA, BCA, B. Sc., B.Com. etc. Undergraduate Admissions is primarily based on academic merit. For B.E. admission students need to have a valid GUJCET or JEE Main score. B. Arch applicants need to present a valid NATA score.

Marwadi University M.E., MBA, M.Sc., and several PG Diploma programs in the postgraduate level. Most postgraduate admission at the university is on the basis of academic merit. However, M.E. admission is on the basis of GATE score, while selection for MBA admission is on the basis of CAT/ XAT/ MAT/ ATMA/ CMAT scores.

Engineering, MBA, MCA and Architecture Admission into the seats earmarked for the state quota is done through the Admission Committee for Professional Courses (ACPC) under the Gujarat Technological University (GTU) and the Govt. of Gujarat (ACPC).

Marwadi University also offers Ph.D. in several disciplines like Engineering, Management, Computer Science, Mathematics, etc. Students can pursue the program in both Full Time and Part Time modes.
